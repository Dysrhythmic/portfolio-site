Remedy

A Discord bot made with Nostrum for playing audio from various sources (e.g. YouTube videos, Twitch streams, local files, HTTP streams, etc.) in voice channels.

Remedy is written in Elixir to increase fault-tolerance through concurrency by taking advantage of the BEAM virtual machine and OTP framework.

Remedy is Dockerized for easier dependency management.
